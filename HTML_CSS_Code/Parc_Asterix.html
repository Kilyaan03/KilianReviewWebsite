Parc Asterix Review

Fun Facts

Parc Astérix is a theme park located in Plailly, France, about 35 kilometers north of Paris.

It is based on the popular Astérix comics created by Albert Uderzo and René Goscinny.

The park opened in 1989.

Parc Astérix features a variety of attractions, including roller coasters, water rides, and live shows.

One of its most famous roller coasters is OzIris, an inverted roller coaster with a unique layout.

The park is divided into several themed areas, each representing a different aspect of the Astérix universe.

Parc Astérix also offers a range of dining options and shops selling Astérix merchandise.

It is one of the most popular theme parks in France.

Best Rides

Parc Astérix is a fantastic theme park, and while I enjoy all the attractions, some rides definitely stand out as my favourites. Here's a quick review of a couple of them:

OzIris: This inverted roller coaster is absolutely incredible! The theming around the Egyptian god Osiris is really well done, and the ride itself is intense. The twists, turns, and inversions are perfectly choreographed, and the sense of speed is exhilarating. Definitely a must-ride for any thrill-seeker visiting the park.

Tonnerre 2 Zeus: A classic wooden coaster that delivers a wild and bumpy ride. It's fast, it's loud, and it's full of airtime. The feeling of the wooden structure and the sheer intensity of the ride make it a truly memorable experience. Definitely one of my favourite wooden coasters anywhere.

Le Grand Splatch: A classic log flume that's perfect for cooling off on a hot day. It's a fun and exciting ride with a couple of decent drops. The theming is also well done, and it's a great ride for the whole family to enjoy.

Overall, Parc Astérix offers a great selection of rides, but these are the ones I always make sure to go on. Whether you're a thrill-seeker or prefer something a bit more relaxed, there's something for everyone to enjoy at Parc Astérix.

Rating: 5/5
